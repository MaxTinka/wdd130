Tinka Foodhub Website Plan

Plan for the online presence of Tinka Foodhub

1. Site Name

Tinka Foodhub

The name "Tinka Foodhub" reflects the restaurant’s focus on offering a diverse range of food from various cultures, with an emphasis on creating a warm, communal dining experience for everyone.

Optional Domain Availability: tinkafoodhub.com

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of Tinka Foodhub's website is to provide customers with a complete online experience, including access to the menu, information about the restaurant, and the ability to make reservations.

The site will feature:

  • A menu section showcasing the restaurant's offerings, including descriptions and pricing.
  • A contact page with the restaurant's location, phone number, and map.
  • A reservations system or form where customers can book a table.
  • An "About Us" section describing the history and philosophy behind Tinka Foodhub.

3. Scenarios

Here are two common scenarios that a visitor might have when interacting with the website:

  • Scenario 1: "What are the restaurant's opening hours?"
  • Scenario 2: "How can I book a reservation for my upcoming dinner?"

4. Color Scheme

The color scheme of the website will be warm and inviting, reflecting the restaurant’s cozy ambiance. Below are the two primary colors:

Primary Color: #c97b5b (Warm brown tone)

This color will be used for headings, navigation background, and accents throughout the site.

Secondary Color: #f4e1c1 (Soft cream color)

This color will be used for background areas, such as sections and footers, to create contrast with the primary color.

5. Typography

The website will use the following fonts:

  • Heading Font: "Georgia" – Used for all main headings (h1, h2) to provide an elegant, sophisticated feel.
  • Body Font: "Arial" – Used for all body text, paragraphs, and menu descriptions for readability.
