History

Founded in 2024, White Water Rafting has been at the forefront of white water rafting in Uganda, providing exhilarating experiences for adventurers of all ages.

Our journey began with a small group of passionate rafters and has grown into a leading adventure company that has been nominated and awarded around East Africa for our wonderful services.
